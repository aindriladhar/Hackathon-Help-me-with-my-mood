How Mind Shift Works

Mind Shift is entirely dependent on twitter timeline. Upon giving permissions (see terms of use), Mind Shift accesses the user's public tweets and then performs sentiment analysis on them. Based on the findings it suggests various things like music, videos, web articles, etc.

1 Get you twitter username and access public tweets.
2 Perform Analysis to find vital seniments.
3 Recommend music, videos, etc. based on your mood.
